⚙️ SharePoint Integration

⚪ Not Connected

All data — glitches and users — is stored in your M365 SharePoint site. Works for all staff on the same domain with no external accounts needed.

📋 One-time setup:
1. Upload glitch-report-sharepoint.html to a SharePoint Document Library on your M365 site
2. Open it directly from SharePoint (not from your desktop)
3. Log in as IT, enter your Site URL below, click Save & Enable
4. Click Auto-Create Lists — the app will build the database for you automatically
5. Done — share the SharePoint link with all staff
